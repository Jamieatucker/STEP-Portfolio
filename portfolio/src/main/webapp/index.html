Hello! My name is Jamie Tucker (Pronouns: he/him/his) and I am a rising Sophomore at The Ohio State University. I'm currently majoring in Computer Science Engineering, learning the necessary skills to become a software engineer.

I've been coding since middle school, starting with drag and drop programs such as Scratch. I built a couple of game projects at various summer camps and even a dance video! As I got older, I experimented with other coding websites, such as Khan Academy and Codecademy, to learn the basics of Java. During my Junior year of high school, I enrolled into a tech campus called Oakland Schools Technical Campus (OSTC), where I learned to build websites using HTML and CSS. This tech campus sparked my passion to pursue a career in Computer Science with their interactive engagement, fun projects, and overall supportive staff.

Below are a few projects that I've built:

  • Pacific Trails Resort Website (OSTC, September 2017)
  • Pokemon Battle Simulator (OSTC, May 2018)
  • Stickman: On The Run Video Game (Freelance Camp, August 2018)
  • Financial Literacy Simulator (OSU Hackathon, November 2019)
  • Text-Based Hang Man (Personal Project, May 2020)
